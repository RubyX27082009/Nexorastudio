Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know before working with us.

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically, a business website takes 1–3 weeks depending on the scope. Landing pages can be done in 3–5 days. E-commerce stores may take 3–5 weeks. We always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do you provide hosting and domain registration?

Yes! We handle everything including domain name purchase and hosting setup on your behalf. You pay one price and we take care of all the technical details so you don't have to stress about anything.

Can I update my website myself after launch?

Yes. For clients who want to manage their own content we build with a simple system that allows easy updates without any coding knowledge. We also provide a brief training session after handover.

What if I need changes after the website is launched?

We provide free support for the period included in your plan. After that we offer affordable monthly maintenance packages to handle any updates, edits, or changes you need.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. All communication and project management is done remotely via WhatsApp, email, and video calls. Payment options for international clients are also available.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just your business name, logo if available, the services you offer, your contact details, and any specific ideas you have for your website. We handle everything else from there!
